Marik Ishtar

Marik was born into the Ishtar family, a clan that guarded the Pharaohâ€™s secrets beneath the sands of Egypt. Forced into this duty from childhood, Marik grew to resent the Pharaoh and his destiny. This hatred led to the creation of his dark alter ego, Yami Marik, born from his anger and suffering. Marik leads the Rare Hunters, a criminal organization that steals rare Duel Monsters cards, and he enters the Battle City Tournament to claim the power of the Pharaoh and the Egyptian God Cards. Despite his cruelty and obsession with revenge, Marik eventually overcomes his dark side with help from Yugi and his friends, finding peace and freedom from his hatred.
